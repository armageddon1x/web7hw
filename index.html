Welcome!


Science and techology are important things to our daily lives. Research and development in these areas have brought advancements such as cell phones, GPS, and supercomputers. Astronomy in particular has not only advanced our knowledge of the universe, but also through overcoming obstacles in sending spacecraft to other worlds, has caused many new technologies to be modified and invented, which have been adapted for the use of the public. It is important to not only keep advancing these scientific endeavors, but also make sure their funding is adequate so that we too may enjoy the fruits of scientists' and engineers' labor.


Astronomy and You


The study of astronomy is the study of the stars (Greek astron, "star" + nomos, "law", which makes "the law of the stars"). Astronomy is one of, if not, the oldest sciences. It was based primarily on meticulous observation by the ancients, and they determined eclipses and other celestial phenomena very accurately. Astronomy influenced culture and religion, such as when to plant crops and when to give offerings to the gods. It has become more sophisticated up until the modern age, but even now still holds bountiful secrets for us to find and ponder over.
